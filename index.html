Top locations to visit in Nainital

Here is a little demo of our services where we give you insights of the places you can visit in th city of Uttrakhand, Nainital




1. The Naina or Cheena peak

The Naina peak is an 8km long trek, Cheena Peak is where trek enthusiast find life. It provides the bird eye view to the beautiful lake city, the Himalayas and also the vast expanses of the Kumaon region. This is a place where many locals will recommend you to visit and the peace around you will surely give an aethetically mesmerizing feeling.

2. The Snow Point

The Snow View Point does justice to its name and offers a majestic view of these hilltops making it one of the most visited sightseeing places in Nainital. The 2,270 m high altitude makes this destination a gateway to the snowy valleys and mountains of the Great Himalayas. You can also enjoy the ride of a cable car that will take you up to this point.

3. The Nainital Mall Road

The Mall Road in Nainital is synonymous to the city itself. It is the most popular tourist destinations in Nainital and most visited one too. Running parallel to the Naini Lake, the Mall Road is 1.5 km long and connects the two ends of Nainital town. It serves as the prime location for culture, food and shopping for the visitors from all over the world.

4. Sherwood Cliffs

A lesser known, but a great trek in Nainital is that of the Sherwood Cliffs. It is about a 3 kms trek and one will find a different level of peace, serenity and tranquility in the place. The place is again a paradise for trek lovers as it is a pure trek through the forests of Nainital and the landsacpe which has the view of St.Joseph's and Raj Bhawan Naintal.
